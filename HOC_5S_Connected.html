Red Tag Process
🔴 Red Tag Procedure
1. Identify: Walk the area and mark any item that does not clearly belong using a red tag.
2. Tag: Write the item description, location, date, and reason on the tag.
3. Quarantine: Move tagged items to the Red Tag holding area within 24 hrs.
4. Decide: Disposition within 30 days — Keep, Relocate, Sell, Recycle, or Discard.
5. Document: Log all red tag actions. Feed results into the Sort pillar score.
