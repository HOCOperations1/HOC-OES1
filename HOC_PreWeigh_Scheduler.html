How to update this app
This app reads batch data baked in at generation time.
To refresh: upload HOC_Production_Tracker_FINAL.xlsx to Claude and say "regenerate". Claude outputs a new version of this file.
Chemical Pulls data: re-export from Vicinity weekly and upload to Claude with "update chemical pulls".
